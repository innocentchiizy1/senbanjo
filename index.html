BACKGROUND & CAREER

Meet Segun Olumide Senbanjo (SOS)… Olusegun Sebanjo is a Renowned businessman and Philanthropist, a man of the people, who uses his resources to Feed the less privileged, helping students to Pay school fees , organized the distribution of foot-ware to primary and secondary school pupils. Olusegun Olumide Senbanjo is friendly, helpful and willing to see the good qualities in people. He is generous and down to earth. Olusegun Senbanjo is one man who has paid his dues full and sure deserves all the accolades he is receiving.

OLUSEGUN OLUMIDE SENBANJO(S0S) was born to the Sogidi family linage of the Awujala of Ijebu Royal crown, Ogun State on the 31st August 1961.
Senbanjo is the true native and son of the soil.
He has a bachelor’s degree in economics from the University of Wolverhampton, England in 1986.
He is married and their marriage is blessed with a son.

Olusegun Senbanjo started his early career at Wintrust Stockbrokers Limited, Lagos in 1987 -1989, where he was trained and worked as a stockbroker, making the company one of the top stockbrokers in Nigeria. In 2008, he was appointed as Non-Executive Director at Forte Oil Nigeria.
He has played a prominent role in the organization, including being chairman of the finance committee.

He is currently a Director at Cogent Global Energy, Geneva, Switzerland, where he has Contributed significantly to the conception of the Energy Africa Fund launched by Company
Olusegun Senbanjo is currently serving as Non-Executive Director at Salta Infrastructure Nigeria Limited, Lagos, Nigeria as well as Country Director of Nord Oil, Lagos, Nigeria - In Charge of business development, identifying new trading opportunities in for Nigerian crude, using an accumulated network of professionals and considerable expertise in order to execute.

He is the founder and Managing Director at Seymour Energy Consulting Limited, Lagos, Nigeria.
He also Founded Mattwood Indas exporting timber Nigeria and Pioneer of several Companies that locally fabricate French windows in Nigeria
